My favourite anime of all time

1. One Piece

Beautiful storytelling and world-building. Favourite since I was a little child.

2. Hell's Paradise

A heart-touching story of a convicted shinobi who takes on a mission of finding 'the elixir of immortality' just for a chance to see his wife.

3.The saga of Tanya the Evil

Hated by the god for defying him, a man is reborn into a new world where the man is reborn as a young girl named Tanya and forced to participate in a war.
